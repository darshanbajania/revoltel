Real Estate PBX

Our PBX solution

We are happy to provide you the best and right solution for your requirements

Features

  • Phone as extension with Wi-Fi Client: Our PBX supports smart phones as extensions through internet
  • Conference bridge faciltiy: With in-built conference bridge facilty, our PBX allows the manager to have call conference call with employees.
  • SMS alert for check-in & check-out: This feature enables front desk to send instant SMS to guest during check-in and prior to check-out time.
  • Website integration with our PBX and hence once any new enquery comes, We can generate auto calls to clients
  • If you have database of clients, Pre defined voice blast can be done to all the clients with your new offers .
  • IVR: "n" levels of IVR for incoming calls with predefined voice files and can be route based on customer respond.
  • Just dial integration: Auto calls to client on every new enquery and once they answer, it will route to employees.
  • Time based incoming routing: We can route calls based on working hours. On non working hours, it can be pass to voice mail or mobile numbers.
  • PBX enables employees to access the office IP PBX from outside to make STD, ISD and local calls at anytime (DISA). Hence reduces in calling cost.
  • Multiple branches can interconnect in one click
  • In built cdr and call recording of all calls
  • Voice mail to email feature
  • Third party application integration like CRM, ERP etc
  • Remember my calls: If the customer calls back from same number, our PBX will automatically route the calls to the sals person who previously attended the call.
  • Call based on the projects: Single DID for all incoming calls and with IVR we can route to multiple projects
  • User friendly and customize based on your requirement
  • Same PBX you can use for your other business also
